C. David Maxey, M.A.
Licensed Psychologist Associate

Clinical Psychology
for Children, Adolescents and Young Adults
in the Tigard Triangle near I-5
off 72nd Ave. at 217/Kruse Way
or by Telehealth Video Call

C. David Maxey, M.A. is a Licensed Psychologist Associate in Independent Practice (Oregon Board of Psychology License 5067). He specializes in Clinical Psychology for children, adolescents, and young adults (approximately ages 4 to 25) and has extensive experience providing mental health services to children and youth over the past {{ site.time | date: "%Y" | minus: 2001 }} years. His evidence-based approach to therapy is individualized to each client’s needs, rather than following to a step-by-step manual-based approach. He incorporates scientifically supported treatment modalities, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (TF-CBT), Parent Management Training (PMT), and Collaborative and Proactive Solutions (formerly known as Collaborative Problem Solving).

New Client Process

1

Use my scheduling link to schedule a free 10-minute phone call.

2

Complete the New Client Request form.

3

To-Do List to Schedule First Appointment

The first appointment is scheduled after you complete the To-Do List items.

Required
  • Upload or email the Developmental History (Fillable) PDF, which can be completed by only one parent.
    When sending copies of papers, please properly scan them with a scanner or app of your choice (e.g., Microsoft Lens on iOS or Android).
  • One or more parents complete the Online Parent Questionnaire.
Recommended
  • Upload or email Records or Reports from other providers (if you have any to share).
    When sending copies of papers, please properly scan them with a scanner or app of your choice (e.g., Microsoft Lens on iOS or Android).
  • Youth 11 years old or older are invited to complete the Online Youth Questionnaire.
4

Expect two emails

after I receive the required items listed above.

  1. email to schedule appointment.
  2. email with a link to a form to sign online.
5

Attend appointments

(in-office or by video call).

First Appointment (up to 90‑120 minutes)

for meeting only with parents—children do not come to the first appointment

Second Appointment (45 minutes)

primarily for meeting your child

Subsequent Appointments (typically 45 minutes)

for meeting with parents, your child, or both, based on your child’s needs

Fees and Insurance

Fees for psychotherapy are $250 for the first appointment and $150 for regular 45-minute appointments. If C. David Maxey, M.A. is in-network with your insurance, the contracted rates are roughly similar, and he can review them with you before the first appointment.

  • If he is in-network with your insurance, he will submit claims to your insurance.
  • If he is out-of-network with your insurance, you can send your insurance a specific type of receipt called a “superbill” that he can provide to you upon request—it includes all the information insurance companies typically need to reimburse you (e.g., dates of service, billing codes, etc.).
  • If you don’t have insurance or you are not using insurance, you have the right to receive a “Good Faith Estimate” explaining how much your health care will cost.

Payment is required on the date of service.

FSA and HSA cards may be accepted for some fees.

Card payments are accepted for psychotherapy appointments as a convenience to you with an additional $5 convenience fee. Cash, check or Zelle (sent through your bank’s app to {{site.email | downcase}}) payments avoid convenience fees. Zelle may not be a private way to pay (e.g., if you enter information in the memo field when sending a payment).

Even if you intend to pay by cash, check or Zelle, a card is required to be stored on file (using HIPAA-secure storage compliant with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards that safeguard card details through encrypted tokenization so that I cannot view stored card details) for expediting payment if ever timely payment by cash, check or Zelle is inconvenient for you.
