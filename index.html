C. David Maxey, M.A.
Licensed Psychologist Associate

Clinical Child & Adolescent
Psychotherapy and Psychological Evaluation
in the Tigard Triangle near I-5
on 72nd Avenue at Kruse Way

I specialize in Clinical Child and Adolescent Psychology, and my approach is rooted in using assessment to thoughtfully select evidence-based treatments, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), behavior management, collaborative problem-solving, and others. I invite you to talk with me about your needs and my services, so we can find out if we might be a good fit. The steps below outline the process.

New Client Process

1
New Client Request: Complete the online New Client Request form★ to provide some basic information.
2
Brief Phone Call: I will email you to schedule an appointment for a free 10-20 minute phone call, so we can briefly discuss followup questions to help us both decide if your needs and my services might be a good fit.

Regular Mental Health Services

3
Forms: Complete online forms★, and submit PDFs through my online file uploader★ or by email to info@cdavidmaxey.com—email may not be a private way to communicate.
  • parent questionnaire online
  • parent questionnaire PDF
  • youth questionnaire online (for youth 11 years old and older)
4
First Appointment: The first appointment is usually up to 90-120 minutes, scheduled during our phone call, and for meeting only with parents—children do not come to the first appointment. This appointment will be automatically cancelled and may not be rescheduled, if forms are not completed by 8am on the day before this appointment.
5
Second Appointment: The second appointment is 45 minutes, scheduled during our phone call or the first appointment, and for meeting with your child. Use my online scheduler★ to request a 45-minute appointment**

EAP (Employee Assistance Program) Services

3
Forms: Complete online forms★, and submit PDFs through my online file uploader★ or by email to info@cdavidmaxey.com—email may not be a private way to communicate.
  • parent questionnaire online
  • parent questionnaire PDF
  • youth questionnaire online (for youth 11 years old and older)
4
First Appointment: The first appointment is 45 minutes, scheduled during our phone call, and for meeting only with parents for at least the beginning of the appointment—your child may join later in the first appointment or the next appointment.

★My online forms and file uploader are powered by a special Microsoft account, HIPAA-secured with a Business Associate Agreement to protect the privacy of your health information.
