Psychological Services

I specialize in clinical child and adolescent psychotherapy and psychological evaluation, and I typically use cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), which is an evidence-based psychotherapy, meaning that it is informed by scientific research. I draw from both classic and contemporary CBT techniques that have research support for their effectiveness. I offer appointments at my office conveniently located in the Tigard Triangle near I-5 on 72nd Avenue at 217.

Fees

My fees for psychotherapy are $175 for the first appointment and $110 for regular 45-minute appointments.

Payment is accepted on the date of service in cash, check, or Zelle sent through your bank’s app to {{site.email | downcase}}. Zelle may not be a private way to pay (e.g., if you enter information in the memo field when sending a payment).

Online Payment+$4 convenience fee

FSA and HSA cards may be accepted online.

Families are asked to store a credit card on file to expedite billing when cash, check or Zelle payment is inconvenient for you. Cards stored on file and online payments (e.g., using the link above) are accepted as a convenience to you with an additional $4 fee.

Forms

Please complete the Questionnaire, and return it to me before your scheduled appointment, along with copies of past evaluations, relevant school information, report cards, standardized test scores, etc. The authorization form is an optional form you can use to allow me to release or obtain information from someone else.
