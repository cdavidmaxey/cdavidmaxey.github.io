Psychological Services

I specialize in psychotherapy and psychological evaluation for school-age children and teens, with a concentration in support for school behavior difficulties, anxiety and depression, challenging behavior at home, and gender-related issues or dysphoria experienced by trans and gender-expansive children and youth. I offer appointments after school hours, on weekends, and over school breaks.

Fees

My fee is $120 per 50-minute session, and I accept full payment in cash, check, or credit card at the time of service.

Forms

New clients can review the following two forms before our first session, so I can fully answer any questions you have before we get started. The last form is an optional form you can use to allow me to release or obtain information from someone else.

Insurance

I’m an out-of-network provider for all insurance companies. I accept full payment in cash, check, or credit card at the time of service, and if you would like to use your out-of-network insurance benefits, I will provide a super bill that you can submit directly to your insurance company or through GetBetter.co, which charges 10% of the money you get paid back—it’s free if your claim is not covered by your insurance or is applied to your deductible.

Suppose:

  • my Standard Fee of $120/session,
  • I am an out-of-network provider,
  • your insurance company has an Allowed Fee of $100, and
  • they cover 60% of the Allowed Fee.

You would have:

  • a copay of $40 (40% of the $100 Allowed Fee) and
  • a remaining fee of $20 (the difference between the Standard Fee and the Allowed Fee).

Therefore, you pay $60/session and your insurance pays $60/session. If you submitted your claim through GetBetter.co, they would charge $6 (10% of the $60 insurance pays), and you would receive $54.
