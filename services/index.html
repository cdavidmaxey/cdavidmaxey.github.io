Psychological Services

I specialize in evidence-based psychotherapy and psychological evaluation for school-age children and teens, with a concentration in support for school behavior difficulties, anxiety and depression, challenging behavior at home, and gender-related issues or dysphoria experienced by trans and gender-expansive children and youth. I offer appointments after school hours, on weekends, and over school breaks at my office in the Tigard Triangle area.

Fees

My fee is $120 per 50-minute session, and I accept full payment in cash, check, or credit card at the time of service.

Insurance

I’m an out-of-network provider for all insurance companies. I accept full payment in cash, check, or credit card at the time of service, and if you would like to use your out-of-network insurance benefits, I will provide a super bill that you can submit directly to your insurance company or through GetBetter.co, which charges 10% of the money you get paid back—there’s no charge if your claim is not covered by your insurance or is applied to your deductible.

You should understand that if you won’t have enough charges to pay off your deductible, usually your insurance won’t cover any of the cost. Note that charges for problems (i.e., diagnoses) that aren’t covered by your insurance, usually don’t count toward paying off your deductible. Unfortunately sometimes the way insurance plans are set up it isn’t all that easy to figure out what will be covered.

Suppose:

  • my Standard Fee of $120/session,
  • I am an out-of-network provider,
  • your insurance company has an Allowed Fee of $100, and
  • they cover 60% of the Allowed Fee.

You would have:

  • a copay of $40 (40% of the $100 Allowed Fee) and
  • a remaining fee of $20 (the difference between the Standard Fee and the Allowed Fee).

Therefore, you would pay $120/session and your insurance would reimburse $60/session. If you submitted your claim through GetBetter.co, they would charge $6 (10% of the $60 insurance pays), and you would receive $54.
