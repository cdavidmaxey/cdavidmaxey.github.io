Psychological Services

I specialize in psychotherapy for children, and I provide appointments after school hours, on the weekend, and over school breaks, because I work during the school day as the Director of Student Support at a local school.

My hourly fee is $120, and I accept full payment in cash or credit card, at the time of service.

Insurance

I’m an out-of-network provider for all insurance companies. I accept full payment in cash or credit card at the time of service, and if you would like to use your out-of-network insurance benefits, I will provide a super bill that you can submit directly to your insurance company or through GetBetter.co, which charges 10% of the money you get paid back—it’s free if your claim is not covered by your insurance or is applied to your deductible.

Suppose:

  • my Standard Fee of $120/session,
  • I am an out-of-network provider,
  • your insurance company has an Allowed Fee of $100, and
  • they cover 60% of the Allowed Fee.

You would have:

  • a copay of $40 (40% of the $100 Allowed Fee) and
  • a remaining fee of $20 (the difference between the Standard Fee and the Allowed Fee).

Therefore, you pay $60/session and your insurance pays $60/session. If you submitted your claim through GetBetter.co, they would charge $6 (10% of the $60 insurance pays), and you would receive $54.
