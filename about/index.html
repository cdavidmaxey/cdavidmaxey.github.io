About
C. David Maxey, M.A.
Licensed Psychologist Associate

C. David Maxey, M.A. is a Licensed Psychologist Associate in Independent Practice (Oregon Board of Psychology License 5067). He received his Master’s in Clinical Psychology with a Concentration in Children and Families from Bowling Green State University and completed a three-year residency through the Children’s Program providing services to children and adolescents. He has extensive broad-ranging experience providing mental health services to children and youth over the past {{ site.time | date: "%Y" | minus: 2001 }} years, and he has maintained a private practice in Tigard since 2018.

Mr. Maxey uses an evidence-based approach towards treatment using modalities such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (TF-CBT), Parent Management Training (PMT), and Collaborative and Proactive Solutions (CPS as known as collaborative problem-solving). He has served as Board President for the Oregon Chapter of the Association for Contextual Behavioral Science, and he is a member of the following organizations:

C. David Maxey uses an initial in place of his first name, Charles, because he goes by David (or Mr. Maxey). During the school year, he offers after-school appointments to his private practice clients, and he works during the school day as the Director of Student Support for a K-8 school, which is separate from his private practice work. Outside of work, he has served on boards of non-profit community organizations, and he enjoys his time with his family including the woman he loves, their children, and the family dog, an affectionate Blenheim Cavalier King Charles Spaniel.
